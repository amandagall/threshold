Who you've been
deserves a proper goodbye.

For the ones who are retiring — this is that moment.

Retirement is one of the most significant moments of your life. For a long time, you were a version of yourself — and now that chapter is closing.

Some people step into what's next with clarity. Others find themselves unmoored. Most feel both.

Threshold is a mirror. A private space to speak honestly about who you've been, what you're carrying, and what you're hoping for.

Over the coming year, Threshold will send you three letters — one before you leave, one when you're finding your footing, and one when you've begun to arrive. Your honest answers will be sealed inside your time capsule.

A year from now, it opens. You'll receive everything you wrote — and meet who you were standing at the threshold. Threshold will be there too, reflecting back how far you've come.

Nobody else will ever read them. This is just for you.
